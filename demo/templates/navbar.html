How to use press-on nails?

1. Prep nails by pushing back cuticles, buffing lightly, and cleansing with the alcohol wipe.
2. Match each press-on to your nail bed and set aside in order.
3. Apply glue or tabs, align carefully, and press for 15 seconds. Instant salon nails!

Do press-ons harm natural nails?

Not when removed properly. Soak nails, slide off gently, and keep your natural nails nourished.
